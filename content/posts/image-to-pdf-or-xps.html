Convert images to PDF or XPS with amazingly easy to use software.

A free, very simple program from CompulsiveCode, Image to PDF or XPS is a very easy to use program for converting single or multiple images to PDF or XPS. Originally developed to convert JPEG images to PDF, it has evolved to encompass the ability to convert images to XPS in addition to PDF, and to provide conversion for almost the entire range of popular image formats.

You can conveniently convert images to PDF plus scale and position them whether their format is JPEG or BMP, TIF, PNG, GIF, WMF, or even XPS, CBZ, and EMF. And, everything is accomplished with a single screen, drag and drop interface that just about everybody will find easy to navigate.

Customize and convert images to XPS or PDF quickly and easily

The key to Image to PDF or XPS software is simplicity. Just download and unzip the free portable utility and start converting. You'll find it a simple matter of selecting images to convert, specifying the settings you want, and saving.

Launched to the single-screen interface, the program enables you to drag and drop images into the Image Files panel at the left, or to browse for images by clicking the Add Files button at the bottom right of the panel. You'll find all your settings options to the right of the panel.

  • Unit of Measure - inches, centimeters, or millimeters radio buttons
  • Output Type - PDF or XPS radio buttons
  • Output File(s) Settings - includes browsable Output Path field, Multiple or Single PDF files and name input for a single PDF
  • Page Size Settings - option to either Match Image Size and Margins or to specify custom Page Size and Margins complete with checkboxes for enlarging or shrinking the Images to Page Size, as well as Centered or Top Left Corner Image Positions buttons.

Should you want to make changes, there are Move and Remove options for your selected files below the Image Files panel and, when everything's set, you'll find the Save button at the bottom right of the interface, and your newly converted file(s) will be saved to the location you've selected.

In addition to using the software's interface, you can convert images to XPS or PDF using the command-line. The EXE file is ImageToPDForXPS.exe which you follow with the names and paths of the new file and the image file(s) to be converted in quotes with a space between each. If you have many images to convert, the program supports the use of an LST file.

A quick and easy solution for when you need to convert images to PDF or XPS, Image To PDF or XPS will definitely prove to be a useful utility to have on your system. Windows OS and Microsoft .Net Framework 3.5 is required.
