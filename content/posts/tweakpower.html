TweakPower cleans and optimizes your computer to leave it running at optimal speed. Installing TweakPower provides you with a tune-up utility that will quickly fine-tune Windows and your applications, refreshing the registry, increasing security, and diagnosing errors as well as potential vulnerabilities. Optimize your computer with TweakPower and both the OS and your software will both load faster and run faster and more smoothly.

TweakPower functions include:

  • Disc Cleanup - Analyzes system, registry, browsers and plug-ins, giving you the option to delete unnecessary files, duplicate files, registry entries, unused plug-ins and internet caches, and to defrag the hard drive.
  • System Settings - Optimizes the computer by customizing the system settings, changing start-up programs, input devices and updating drivers to meet your specific needs.
  • Security - Configures and manages Windows settings including firewall, automatic updates, user administration, and privacy and security center settings. Permanently deletes sensitive data, encrypts files (using Advanced Encryption Standard), safely deletes unneeded files and controls network settings and peripheral devices.
  • System Maintenance - Manages Autostart and Autoplay functions, and scheduled tasks. Uninstalls/changes applications, troubleshoots disk errors and typical Windows problems.
  • Communication - Customizes internet connections and browsers and synchronizes your computer with the atomic clock.

TweakPower allows you to customize Windows - appearance, colors, icons, buttons, as well as your start-up screen, start-up menu and desktop display. You can change your operating mode - from Standard to Economy Mode to reduce the processor's power consumption by switching off unnecessary background processes or to Turbo Mode to turn off unnecessary processes to provide more power for browsing, video and games.

All optimizations are saved and restore points created so that you are enabled to return your system to an earlier state at any time.

Additional features of TweakPower are:

  • Security - File Splitter, File Shredder.
  • Registry - Search and Backup.
  • Hard Disk - Scan Directory Sizes and Restore Deleted Files.
  • Windows - View System Settings, Devices and Utilities.

There are several TweakPower add-ons:

  • Defragment for faster loading of applications, opening and copying files and smoother running of programs and games.
  • TaskManager for displaying processor usage, and managing start-up and running and ending of programs.
  • 1-Click Maintenance for resolving PC problems and automatically improving performance of programs and games.

Conclusion

Essentially, it's not necessary to be a computer expert to clean your PC. Just use TweakPower to quickly remove backup, log and other files that are no longer needed, program installation leftovers and all the browser clutter. Then, use its functions to fine-tune settings to completely optimize your computer.
